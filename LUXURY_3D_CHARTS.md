# Phase 3.4 — Luxury 3D Charts

## Visual upgrades
- Pseudo-3D beveled vertical and horizontal bars
- Raised elliptical donut charts with depth layers and illuminated centre medallions
- Premium line charts with glow, layered area depth and sphere-style data points
- Glass plot backgrounds, perspective grids and soft gold highlights
- Luxury chart-card hover elevation and restrained animated sheen
- Glossy 3D legends and improved visual hierarchy
- Simplified mobile rendering and reduced-motion accessibility

## Safety
This is a frontend-only visual patch. Database, calculations and business records are unchanged.

After installing, restart the app and press **Ctrl + F5** once.
