# Owner Demo Flow — Phase 3.1.1

## 1. Executive Dashboard
- Show All Branches, Al Khoud and Azaiba filters.
- Explain that production output now uses the approved Body/Joint formula.

## 2. Production Formula
- Open Production.
- Select a Body employee.
- Enter Body `10` and Joint/Side `15` together.
- Show the live preview: **10 Full Body Produced**.
- Show Daraz/VIP Design/Iron/Cutting where 10 done means 10 produced.

## 3. Membership Card
- Open Issue Membership Card.
- Enter the existing manual card number.
- Type a sales-agent name directly.
- Show the `+ Add Sales Agent` option.
- Explain the editable 10% commission and monthly commission report.

## 4. Budget Approval
- Select one branch and month.
- Click Approve.
- Click Approve & Lock.
- Show that budget fields become disabled.
- Use Unlock / Draft to enable editing again.

## 5. Users & Permissions
- Open Users & Roles.
- Show the role list.
- Edit Production Supervisor and show Production, Attendance and Orders permissions.
- Demonstrate that a custom role can be added.

## 6. Other Modules
- Orders and delivery tracking.
- Payroll and attendance.
- Editable budgets and alerts.
- Audit log and reports.

## 7. Approval Decision
After the owner confirms the workflow and final changes, deploy the approved build to permanent PostgreSQL hosting.
